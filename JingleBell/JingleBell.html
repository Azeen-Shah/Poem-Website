Jingle Bells Poem

Dashing through the snow
On a one-horse open sleigh
O'er the fields we go
Laughing all the way
Dashing through the snow
On a one-horse open sleigh
O'er the fields we go
Laughing all the way


Jingle bells, jingle bells,
Jingle all the way
Sainta claus is coming along
Riding down this way
Jingle bells, jingle bells,
Jingle all the way
Sainta claus is coming along
Riding down this way
